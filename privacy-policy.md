### Non-Personal Technical Data and Analytics Data

To maintain and improve the App, we may process limited technical and usage information through Google services:

- **Device and App Information:** Device type, operating system version, app version, language, and timezone
- **App Usage Data:** In-app events such as screen views, category selection, level start/completion, and purchase flow events
- **Technical Diagnostics:** Crash information, performance metrics, and reliability diagnostics
- **Identifiers:** App instance identifiers, Google Analytics/Firebase identifiers, and device-level advertising identifiers where available under platform settings
- **Network Data:** IP address and basic connection metadata processed by service providers for delivery, security, and analytics

We do not intentionally collect direct identifiers such as names, personal email addresses, or phone numbers through this App.

### Local Storage and Cloud Analytics

All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored **locally on your device**. This includes:

- Game stage completion status
- User preferences (sound settings, difficulty levels)
- In-app purchase status (premium categories unlock)

Gameplay and purchase telemetry events may be transmitted securely to Google Firebase/Google Analytics for app analytics and performance monitoring.

# Privacy Policy for Kids Memory Match

**Effective Date:** 02/25/2026 
**Last Updated:** 02/25/2026

## Introduction

Kids Memory Match ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our educational memory matching game. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information from users of our mobile application.

---

## Information We Collect

### We Do NOT Collect Personal Information

Kids Memory Match is designed to be a safe, offline educational game for children. We do **NOT** collect, store, or share any personal information from our users, including:

- ❌ Names
- ❌ Email addresses
- ❌ Phone numbers
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Photos or videos
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Any other personally identifiable information

### Non-Personal Information

Our app may collect limited, non-personal technical information automatically, such as:

- Device type (e.g., tablet, phone)
- Operating system version
- App crash reports (for debugging purposes only)

This information is used solely to improve app performance and is **NOT** linked to any individual user.

---

## How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use or share any user data. Any technical information collected is used only to:

- Fix bugs and improve app stability
- Enhance user experience
- Ensure compatibility across devices

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Kids Memory Match is designed for children under the age of 13 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do **NOT**:

- Collect personal information from children
- Require children to provide information to access features
- Enable children to make their information publicly available
- Use behavioral advertising targeted at children

---

## Data Security

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no user data stored on our servers. All game progress and settings are stored locally on the user's device and are never transmitted to us.
